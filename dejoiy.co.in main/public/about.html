About DEJOIY

We are building
the operating
system of enterprise.

DEJOIY India Private Limited is a global enterprise technology company creating the infrastructure that powers commerce, intelligence, and business growth for modern organizations.

Mission

To build the reliable digital infrastructure that simplifies commerce and empowers business users.

We create platforms that make complex enterprise operations simple, fast, and scalable — removing friction from every step of the digital business journey.

Vision

To become the category-defining technology ecosystem connecting enterprises to the future.

A world where every enterprise — regardless of size or geography — has access to the same intelligence, infrastructure, and tools as the world's largest organizations.

Our Story

Born from the belief that technology should serve every enterprise, not just the elite.

DEJOIY was founded with a singular conviction: that the gap between enterprise-grade technology and small-to-mid-market businesses is artificially large, and that a platform built with genuine intelligence could eliminate it.

From our headquarters in India — one of the world's fastest-growing technology ecosystems — we are building infrastructure that doesn't just serve India, but powers organizations across every major market globally.

The DEJOIY ecosystem is designed to be interconnected. Our Marketplace connects commerce. Our Business Portal powers operations. Our AI systems drive intelligence. Our developer ecosystem enables builders. Together, they form the backbone of a new kind of technology empire.
